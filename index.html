Our Story

Jeevan Jyoti Seva Society was founded in 2024 with a deep commitment to serve the marginalized communities across India. Our name, meaning "Light of Life Service Society," reflects our mission to bring hope, dignity, and transformation to those in need.

We are currently working in rural areas and urban slums, providing essential services in education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Rooted in Christian values of love and service, we reach out to all people regardless of background, bringing tangible hope and lasting change.

Through the generous support of donors like you, we continue to expand our reach and deepen our impact in the communities we serve.

Mission

To bring light, hope, and transformative service to underserved communities in India through education, empowerment, and compassionate care.

Vision

A society where every child, woman, and elderly person has access to dignity, opportunity, and comprehensive care.

Our Programs

Comprehensive programs designed to create lasting impact

Education Support

Providing quality education and resources to children from underprivileged families, ensuring they have the tools to succeed.

  • School supplies and uniforms
  • After-school tutoring programs
  • Scholarship opportunities
  • Computer literacy training

Women's Empowerment

Equipping women with vocational skills and business training to achieve financial independence and community leadership.

  • Tailoring and handicraft training
  • Microfinance and business support
  • Leadership development
  • Health and nutrition education

Elderly Care

Providing compassionate care, medical support, and community for senior citizens who lack family support systems.

  • Medical checkups and medications
  • Nutritious meals and supplements
  • Social activities and companionship
  • Home visits and assistance

Emergency Relief

Rapid response to natural disasters and crises, providing immediate aid and long-term recovery support to affected communities.

  • Food and water distribution
  • Emergency shelter and supplies
  • Medical assistance
  • Rebuilding and rehabilitation
